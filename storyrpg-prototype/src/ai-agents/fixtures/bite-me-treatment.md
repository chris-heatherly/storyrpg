# Bite Me — Season One Treatment

A StoryRPG branching-narrative season treatment.

## 7. Episode Outline

### Episode 1 — "Welcome to Dusk"
- **Structural role:** Hook.
- **Tone register:** Sparkly paranormal rom-com with one dark bite at the end.
- **Episode promise:** Can Kylie survive her first fabulous night in Bucharest?
- **Synopsis:** Kylie meets Mika and Stela, launches the Dusk Club, and is rescued by Victor after an attack in the park.
- **Opening situation:** Kylie unpacking in Lipscani with her grandmother's gold chain on the desk.
- **Encounter anchor (light):** The rooftop bar at sunset where the Dusk Club locks into place.
- **Encounter anchor (dark):** Cismigiu at 1am — fog, a shadow, a scream, a rescue.
- **Encounter buildup:** Mika's club invitation, Stela's quartz, and the rooftop gaze make the park attack feel personal.
- **Major choice pressure:**
  - Accept Mika's key card or leave the club untouched.
  - Accept Stela's quartz or refuse the warding invitation.
  - Walk over to Victor or follow Mika's warning.
- **Alternative paths:**
  - Accepting the quartz grants Stela's wards permission and keeps the apartment safer later.
  - Refusing the quartz leaves the apartment vulnerable when Victor escalates.
- **Consequence seeds:** The quartz. Mika's half-second of stillness. The black roses. The blog readership counter.
- **Cliffhanger:** Stela texts that she had a horrible dream and is coming over with herbs.

### Episode 2 — "Mr. Midnight"
- **Structural role:** Plot Turn 1.
- **Tone register:** Dating-column comedy with a velvet shadow.
- **Episode promise:** Who is Mr. Midnight, and how brave is Kylie willing to be?
- **Synopsis:** Kylie goes on terrible dates, meets Victor properly, and meets Radu after a cab breakdown.
- **Opening situation:** Kylie watching the blog dashboard climb.
- **Encounter anchor:** The first long conversation with Victor at Vâlcescu Club.
- **Encounter buildup:** Bad dates and Stela's tarot make Victor feel like the answer to a bad week.
- **Major choice pressure:**
  - Stay at Vâlcescu Club or leave after one drink.
  - Ask Victor about the blog codename or deflect.
  - Accept Radu's lift or wait for the tow.
- **Alternative paths:**
  - Staying late opens the first invitation question at Kylie's apartment door.
  - Accepting Radu's lift seeds his family world earlier.
- **Consequence seeds:** Stela's tarot. Radu's cousins. Victor's line that Kylie can write what she likes for now.
- **Cliffhanger:** A no-profile account warns Kylie that Ileana is missing and that she should not go to Victor's weekend.

### Episode 3 — "The Weekend"
- **Structural role:** Plot Turn 1 completion.
- **Tone register:** Glossy country-house romance with one anonymous warning.
- **Episode promise:** What does Kylie look like when she lets herself be courted?
- **Synopsis:** Kylie spends the weekend at Victor's estate and notices the first impossible cracks.
- **Opening situation:** The drive north with Mika in the back seat and Stela texting warnings.
- **Encounter anchor:** The kiss in the hedge maze at midnight.
- **Encounter buildup:** Victor's attention, the rose garden, the dinner toast, and the unnamed model crying in the powder room.
- **Major choice pressure:**
  - Publish the pre-weekend post or protect Victor's privacy.
  - Drink the dark wine or refuse it.
  - Press Ileana gently or let her go.
- **Alternative paths:**
  - Publishing the post starts the Blog War branch.
  - Drinking the dark wine creates a craving that returns in later episodes.
  - Giving Ileana your number means she can message later.
- **Consequence seeds:** Ileana's warning. The wine craving. Mika's missing hour. The grandmother name slip.
- **Cliffhanger:** Radu's scarf appears on Kylie's doormat even though she never gave him her address.

### Episode 4 — "The Mountain"
- **Structural role:** Pinch 1.
- **Tone register:** Woodsmoke second-lead romance with gathering unease.
- **Episode promise:** Is there a version where Kylie chooses the quiet life?
- **Synopsis:** Kylie visits Radu's lodge while Mika and Stela pull her in opposite directions.
- **Opening situation:** Kylie driving north with Radu's scarf beside her.
- **Encounter anchor:** Fire-lit dinner at Casa Lupului.
- **Encounter buildup:** A Dusk Club debate, Radu's cousins, and Stela's new-moon candle request.
- **Major choice pressure:**
  - Tell Mika and Stela everything about Victor or downplay it.
  - Answer Radu honestly or deflect.
  - Let Stela candle-ward the apartment or refuse.
- **Alternative paths:**
  - Telling only Stela starts her investigation earlier.
  - Saying yes to Stela's candle strengthens the apartment ward.
  - Staying at the lodge softens Radu's later confession.
- **Consequence seeds:** The wooden charm. Radu's cousins. Stela's candle. The blog discussion email.
- **Cliffhanger:** The no-profile account tells Kylie to ask The Mountain about his last girlfriend.

### Episode 5 — "Mirror, Mirror"
- **Structural role:** Midpoint.
- **Tone register:** Romance until the genre flips in real time.
- **Episode promise:** What does Kylie want when she finally lets herself want?
- **Synopsis:** Victor asks Kylie to wind down the blog; later, the mirror reveals what he is.
- **Opening situation:** Kylie dressing for Victor while FaceTiming Sadie.
- **Encounter anchor (dark):** The mirror moment in Victor's bedroom.
- **Encounter anchor (warm):** Stela's bookshop floor at 3am.
- **Encounter buildup:** A tender lunch, the blog offer, and Stela's Tower card.
- **Major choice pressure:**
  - Agree to consider winding down the blog or refuse.
  - Confront Victor, run, or pretend you did not see.
  - Hear all of Stela's truth or shut the night down.
- **Alternative paths:**
  - Agreeing to wind down the blog softens Victor now but strengthens his claim later.
  - Pretending you did not see points toward the Hollowed failure state.
  - Writing a coded post can pull Victor into the apartment if wards are weak.
- **Consequence seeds:** The mirror truth. Stela's family. The first missed blog day. Veronica's photograph.
- **Cliffhanger:** Kylie sees Victor's stag-crest ring in her grandmother's 1961 photograph as Victor texts, "We should talk."

### Episode 6 — "Wolf at the Door"
- **Structural role:** Pinch 2.
- **Tone register:** Dark romance with a friend group still holding the frame.
- **Episode promise:** Is anyone in Kylie's life just a person?
- **Synopsis:** Radu confesses what he is, Carmen is attacked, and Victor frames Radu.
- **Opening situation:** Kylie laying quartz, herbs, and her grandmother's chain on the kitchen table.
- **Encounter anchor:** Radu's kitchen confession.
- **Encounter buildup:** Mika's tearful call, Stela's silent drive, and Carmen's DMs.
- **Major choice pressure:**
  - Be comforted by Mika or notice her gaps.
  - Walk out on Radu or sit longer.
  - Publish Don't Tell Me What to Write or take it down.
- **Alternative paths:**
  - Noticing Mika shifts the next reveal from betrayal to confession.
  - Publishing the post breaks the blog war fully open.
- **Consequence seeds:** Carmen. Radu's framing. Mika cracking. The angry post.
- **Cliffhanger:** A black rose waits inside Kylie's warded apartment with Victor's card saying Mika brought it.

### Episode 7 — "Best Friend"
- **Structural role:** Pinch 2 completion.
- **Tone register:** Friendship reckoning inside dark romance.
- **Episode promise:** Can Kylie hold on to the friend she loves after the truth?
- **Synopsis:** Kylie confronts Mika and learns she has been steering Kylie toward Victor.
- **Opening situation:** The black rose in a plastic bag on Stela's counter.
- **Encounter anchor:** Kylie versus Mika in empty Drăgan Vintage at midnight.
- **Encounter buildup:** Stela's briefing and Kylie's afternoon reading every text Mika ever sent.
- **Major choice pressure:**
  - Help free Mika, cut her off, or ask for time.
  - Call in Stela's family or attempt the contract alone.
  - Publish the nuclear draft post or leave it in drafts.
- **Alternative paths:**
  - Helping Mika opens the Witness ending.
  - Publishing the draft post forces Victor's hand at the climax.
  - Calling Stela's family brings a sledgehammer to the finale.
- **Consequence seeds:** Mika's confession. The salt circle. Radu's message. Carmen's spreadsheet. The draft post.
- **Cliffhanger:** Radu stands in the courtyard and admits he tried to be there the first night.

### Episode 8 — "Bite Me"
- **Structural role:** Climax and Resolution.
- **Tone register:** Full dark romance with the warmth of the Dusk Club in one ending.
- **Episode promise:** Who is Kylie Marinescu now?
- **Synopsis:** The Hunter's Moon ball forces Kylie to choose what she will and will not become.
- **Opening situation:** Kylie dressing for Casa Stelarum at sunset.
- **Encounter anchor:** Casa Stelarum at moonrise — three factions, three offers, one speech.
- **Encounter buildup:** Carmen publishes, Stela lights candles, Mika chooses a coat, and Victor waits.
- **Major choice pressure:**
  - Whose hand do you take into the ballroom?
  - Stand beside Mika or with Victor during the salt circle.
  - Give Victor the blog or refuse him.
- **Alternative paths:** This episode is the convergence point of the season's branches.
- **Consequence seeds:** The Strigoi Mama. Sadie's visit. Mika's freedom. Veronica's letter.
- **Cliffhanger / closing image:** Kylie writes the final blog post at dawn; the first sentence changes by ending.

## 8. Cross-Episode Branches And Consequence Chains

### Branch A — The Quartz (Episode 1 → Episode 5 reconvergence)
Accepting the quartz grants Stela's wards permission. Refusing leaves the apartment vulnerable. Reconverges at the mirror moment in ep 5.

### Branch B — The Blog War (Episode 3 → Episode 8 reconvergence)
Publishing, refusing, or escalating blog posts changes Victor's pressure and which ending routes stay healthy. Reconverges in the ballroom.

### Branch C — The Country-House Wine (Episode 3 → Episode 7 reconvergence)
Drinking the dark wine creates a craving that returns as a hand tremor. Refusing leaves no appetite but is noticed.

### Branch D — Mika's Crossroad (Episode 1 → Episode 8 reconvergence)
Reading Mika gently and early shifts her loyalty toward Kylie. Reading cruelly returns Mika to Victor. Reconverges at the salt circle.

### Branch E — The Mountain Confession (Episode 4 → Episode 6 reconvergence)
Staying with Radu and sitting after the confession opens the Mountain Wife route.

## 9. Alternate Endings

### Ending 1 — "The Last Sunrise" (The Consort)
**Summary:** Kylie accepts Victor's offer, winds down the blog, and is turned at Casa Stelarum.
**Emotional register:** Tragic-glamorous.
**Theme payoff:** Being adored is not the same as keeping your voice.
**State drivers:** Victor-aligned choices. Blog wind-down agreement. Dark wine. Mika not freed.
**Target conditions:** Victor-aligned pattern across five major choices. The quartz is refused or lost.

### Ending 2 — "Howl Through the Carpathians" (The Mountain Wife)
**Summary:** Kylie chooses Radu, helps publish the spreadsheet, and leaves the city for an honest mortal life.
**Emotional register:** Bittersweet-warm.
**Theme payoff:** Truth can be less glamorous and more freeing than adoration.
**State drivers:** Radu-aligned choices. Staying at Casa Lupului. Publishing against Victor. Refusing the wine.
**Target conditions:** Radu-aligned pattern across five major choices. The quartz is accepted and kept.

### Ending 3 — "Dating After Dusk" (The Witness)
**Summary:** Kylie chooses neither man, frees Mika, keeps the blog, and claims the Marinescu line.
**Emotional register:** Triumphant-warm.
**Theme payoff:** The happy ending is voice, friendship, and the truth.
**State drivers:** Self-and-friends choices. Hearing Stela. Reading Mika gently. Refusing Victor.
**Target conditions:** Self-aligned pattern across five major choices. Mika is freed. The blog survives.
